Vatsalya Nagar

Vatsalya Nagar is a proposed city in Madhya Pradesh designed to embody the spirit of affection and community. Rooted in the values of togetherness and warmth, the city will promote social interaction, cultural exchange, and sustainable living.

Overview

  • Area: 1500 sq. km (370,000 acres)
  • Location: Madhya Pradesh, India
  • Capital Required: ₹8,00,000 crores (~100 billion USD)

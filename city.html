Marina Bay

"Marina Bay" a proposed city in Pondicherry envisions a futuristic city built upon the principles of sustainability, innovation, and citizen-centric design. Inspired by the concept of smart cities, Marina Bay aims to leverage technology to enhance the quality of life for its residents while minimizing environmental impact.

Overview

  • Area: 1500 sq. km (370,000 acres)
  • Location: Pondicherry, India
  • Capital Required: ₹9,800 crores
